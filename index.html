Route Plan — US Highway 1 → US Highway 99/I-5

Culver City → Beaverton

A Pacific-coast crawl up Highway 1 through Big Sur and Monterey Bay, a redwood detour past San Francisco, then an inland run up Highway 99 and I-5 into Oregon, finishing with one last stop at the coast before Beaverton.
